# FIFA World Cup 2026 — MetLife Stadium Rules & Entry Requirements

## General Entry Policy
- Gates open 2.5 hours before kickoff for all ticket holders.
- All guests must present a valid ticket (digital or printed) and government-issued photo ID.
- Re-entry is permitted at Gates A and C only, within 90 minutes of first entry.
- Children under 3 years of age do not require a ticket but must be accompanied by a ticketed adult.

## Prohibited Items
The following items are strictly prohibited inside MetLife Stadium:
- Bags larger than 12"x6"x12" (exceptions: medical bags, diaper bags with infant present)
- Glass containers, cans, or hard-sided coolers
- Alcoholic beverages from outside the stadium
- Drones, selfie sticks, tripods, or professional recording equipment
- Laser pointers, flares, fireworks, or any pyrotechnic device
- Weapons of any kind, including knives, firearms, or pepper spray
- Oversized banners or flags that obstruct views of other guests
- Inflatable items, noise makers exceeding stadium decibel limits

## Permitted Items
- Clear bags up to 12"x6"x12" or small clutch purses (4.5"x6.5")
- One sealed, factory-bottled water (up to 20 oz) per guest
- Medically necessary items (insulin pens, EpiPens, hearing aids) with proof if required
- Folding umbrellas (no golf umbrellas)
- Binoculars without a case
- Official FIFA merchandise, flags under 2 feet x 3 feet

## Security Screening
- All guests must pass through metal detector wands or walk-through security arches.
- Bags are subject to visual inspection and X-ray scanning.
- Security lanes open 3 hours before kickoff. Guests with accessible needs should use the designated accessible security lane at each gate.
- K-9 units may be present for explosive detection.

## Code of Conduct
- Guests are expected to conduct themselves in a respectful manner at all times.
- Abusive, threatening, or discriminatory language will result in immediate ejection.
- Intoxicated guests will be refused entry or removed from the stadium.
- Any guest causing a disruption will be removed by stadium security without a refund.
- FIFA's Fan Code of Conduct applies to all attendees.

## Alcohol Policy
- Beer and wine are sold at licensed concession stands throughout the stadium.
- Last call for alcohol is at 75 minutes into the match (approximately 15 minutes before full time).
- Proof of age (21+) is required for alcohol purchases in the USA.
- Guests visibly intoxicated will not be served.

## Smoking Policy
- MetLife Stadium is a smoke-free facility.
- Smoking, including e-cigarettes and vaping, is prohibited inside the stadium and on stadium grounds.
- Designated smoking areas are located in the main parking lot, at least 100 feet from the nearest gate entrance.

## Ticket & Refund Policy
- Tickets are non-refundable and non-transferable (except via FIFA's official transfer portal).
- In case of match postponement, tickets will be valid for the rescheduled date.
- Counterfeit tickets will result in denial of entry and possible law enforcement involvement.

## Photography & Media
- Personal photography for non-commercial use is permitted.
- Professional cameras with removable lenses or video cameras are prohibited unless you have a valid media credential.
- Live streaming of the match is prohibited under FIFA broadcast rights agreements.

## Lost & Found
- The Lost & Found desk is located at Gate A (North Concourse), Level 1.
- After the event, lost items can be claimed via the MetLife Stadium website within 30 days.
- Lost children should be reported immediately to any security officer or the Family Reunification Point near Gate C.

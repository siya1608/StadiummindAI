## SPECIAL SERVICES & DIETARY ASSISTANCE

### Allergen & Dietary Info Desk
- Location: Adjacent to Global Grills, Level 2 South (near Section 224)
- Staff can provide complete ingredient lists and allergen guides for all concessions.
- Pre-ordered special dietary meals (Halal, Kosher) can be collected here with advance booking confirmation.

### Halal & Kosher Options
- Pre-order required via MetLife Stadium's event portal at least 48 hours before the match.
- Pick-up at Allergen & Dietary Info Desk (Level 2, near Section 224).
- Halal: Grilled chicken wrap, beef hot dog (both Halal certified).
- Kosher: Kosher beef hot dog, sealed fruit cups, kosher chips.

### Express Mobile Ordering
- Available via the MetLife Stadium App for Sections 100–150, 200–248.
- Order is delivered to your seat or ready at an express pickup counter.
- Average delivery time: 12–18 minutes during peak demand.

### Water Stations
- Free water refill stations located at:
  * Level 1: Near Gate A (Section 101) and Gate C (Section 129)
  * Level 2: Near Section 201 and Section 238
  * Level 3: Near Section 315 and Section 335

### Concession Peak Times
- Highest congestion: 30 minutes before kickoff and half-time (approximately minutes 40-55).
- Recommended: Order via mobile app or visit Level 3 stalls which experience 40% less wait time vs Level 1 and Level 2.

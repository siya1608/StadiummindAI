=== STADIUM OVERVIEW ===
MetLife Stadium is a multi-purpose stadium located in East Rutherford, New Jersey. It has a seating capacity of 82,500 for soccer/football and is a host venue for the FIFA World Cup 2026. The stadium layout consists of three primary seating tiers: Lower Tier (100 Level), Plaza/Mezzanine Tier (200 Level), and Upper Tier (300 Level).

=== SEATING SECTIONS AND GATES ===
- Gate A (North Concourse): Easiest access for Sections 101-115, 201-208, and 301-315.
- Gate B (East Concourse): Easiest access for Sections 116-128, 209-220, and 316-328. This area is known to experience heavy traffic during entry and exit times.
- Gate C (South Concourse): Easiest access for Sections 129-143, 221-232, and 329-343.
- Gate D (West Concourse): Easiest access for Sections 144-150, 233-248, and 344-350.
- West Wing A1 Camera covers the West Wing entrance concourses, providing real-time feed for security.

=== ACCESSIBILITY & WHEELCHAIR ROUTES ===
- MetLife Stadium is fully accessible. Accessible seating (wheelchair and companion seats) is available on all levels: Lower Level (Sections 109, 117, 124, 135, 142, 149), Mezzanine Level (Sections 201, 207A, 220A, 224A, 230, 245A), and Upper Level (Sections 316, 324, 335, 342, 349).
- Elevators: There are 10 public passenger elevators located throughout the stadium:
  * Plaza/Gate A: Near Section 101.
  * Plaza/Gate B: Near Section 117.
  * Plaza/Gate C: Near Section 128.
  * Plaza/Gate D: Near Section 144.
- Ramps: Main pedestrian ramps are located at the North and South corners of the stadium.
- Low Vision Mode / Sensory Rooms: Located at Plaza Level Section 109 for families and guests needing sensory relief.

=== CONCESSIONS & FOOD RECOMMENDATIONS ===
- Food Court Level 2 South (near Section 224): Features "Global Grills" (FIFA specials, hot dogs, tacos), "Apex Vegan Burgers" (100% plant-based burgers, fries), and "Gluten-Free Zone" (GF buns, salads, snacks).
- Concourse Level 1 (near Section 117): Features "MetLife Eats" (standard burgers, chicken tenders, beers).
- Concourse Level 1 (near Section 142): Features "Jersey Shore Eats" (cheese steaks, boardwalk fries).
- Concourse Level 3 (near Section 315): Features "Skyline Snacks" (pretzels, popcorn, soda).
- Concourse Level 3 (near Section 338): Features "Upper Deck Dogs" (premium hot dogs, beers).

=== TRANSPORT & PARKING ===
- Parking Lots:
  * Main Lot (Lots E, F, G): Closest to Gate A and B. Reserved for VIP, staff, and general pre-paid parking.
  * East Lot (Lots P, Q): Closest to Gate B.
  * South Lot (Lots K, L): Closest to Gate C.
  * Rideshare Pick-up/Drop-off zone: Located in Lot D.
- Public Transit:
  * NJ Transit Rail Service: Trains run directly from Secaucus Junction to the Meadowlands Sports Complex Station (located adjacent to Gate C).
  * Shuttles (MetLife Express): Runs regularly from parking lot P to Gate C and Gate A. Buses arrive approximately every 4-5 minutes.

=== EMERGENCY PROCEDURES ===
- In case of a medical emergency, Zone 4 First Aid Station is located on the Plaza Level near Section 109. There are also first aid stations near Section 144, Section 224, and Section 315.
- Average response/wait time for Medical Zone 4 Station is 2 minutes.
- To contact Stadium Security during an event, guests can text "MED" or "SEC" followed by their issue and seat location to 84444, or call the in-stadium security assistance line at *911.
- In case of an evacuation, follow instructions from stadium staff and exit through the nearest gate (Gate A, B, C, or D). Do not use elevators during an evacuation.
